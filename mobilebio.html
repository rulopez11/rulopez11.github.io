    VIIER is an up and coming progressive house group based out of Southern California. The group is comprised of a Latin trio looking to make their mark in the dance music scene and provide a voice for their underrepresented Latin American community. The trio utilizes elegant melodies infused with explosive drums and energetic bass lines. Their production process is deeply influenced by Latin culture and their Southern California upbringing. VIIER draws inspiration from the ideal of creating your destiny and charting your own path no matter where your'e from, your situation, or who you are.
